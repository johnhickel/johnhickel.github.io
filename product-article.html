Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

This is the 6th installment in the Super Smash Bros. series. The premier fighting game franchise on Nintendo consoles has returned with its biggest game yet. More characters, more stages, and more ways to play than ever before.

The franchise started with Super Smash Bros. 64 in 1999 and the series has sold over 70 million units total. With Nintendo characters like Mario, Sonic, Samus, Link, and Kirby and 3rd party characters like Steve (Minecraft), Sephiroth, Cloud, and Banjo & Kazooie the gigantic scope of the game can be seen. Taking influence from gaming classics with characters like Pac-Man to new hits like Joker (Persona) the game has characters that everyone will enjoy playing.

All of this cumulates in one gigantic game that has a story mode, local and online PvP, and PvE which gives players many options on how to enjoy the game.
